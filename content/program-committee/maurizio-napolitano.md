Maurizio Napolitano, known as napo, is a technologist at the Bruno Kessler
Foundation in Trento, where he directs the Digital Commons Lab (DCL).
With DCL works on the topics of open data, digitals commons and data governance.
He is also a strong supporter of OpenStreetMap and a lecturer in Geospatial
Analysis for the Master in Data Science at the University of Trento
